1. Introduction

Welcome to DMorbit ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you use our Instagram automation service.

2. Information We Collect

When you use DMorbit, we collect the following information:

  • Communication Data: Messages sent through our automation system and verification interactions.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and maintain our Instagram automation services

4. Data Storage and Security

We take data security seriously:

  • Your data is stored securely using industry-standard encryption

  • We regularly review and update our security practices

We may share your information only in these specific circumstances:

We explicitly do NOT:

Through Instagram's API, we access:

We explicitly do NOT:
